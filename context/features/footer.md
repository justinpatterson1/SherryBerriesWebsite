## 10. Footer

A 5-column footer with brand block (logo, paragraph, social icons, payment chips) plus 4 link columns, separated by a thin line from a 2-row baseline (copyright + legal links).

### Recreation prompt
> Build a footer with a near-black background (`#050505`) and a 1px subtle top border. Inside: a 5-column grid (1.5fr 1fr 1fr 1fr 1fr — stacks 2-col below 980px) containing: (1) a brand column with the SherryBerries wordmark logo, a 320px-max short description ("Luxury body jewelry & piercing aftercare — made with love in Brooklyn & Bridgetown."), 4 circular social icons (Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube) that glow pink on hover, and 4 small payment badge pills (VISA, MC, AMEX, AFTERPAY); (2–5) four link columns headed "Shop", "Care", "Help", "Follow" with 11px uppercase 0.18em-letter-spaced blush headers and 14px regular muted links that turn blush on hover. Below the grid: a thin 1px line separator, then a bottom row split between "© 2026 SherryBerries Atelier · Made with ♡ in Brooklyn & Bridgetown" on the left and three legal links (Privacy, Terms, Accessibility) on the right.
